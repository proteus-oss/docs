Proteus

A Groovy DSL to safely run Cassandra migrations.

Safe

With Proteus, you can control what types of operations are allowed and when they can be run. Unsafe operations can be disallowed.

Repeatable

Proteus allows you to see and control what changes are applied.

Extensible

Proteus is extensible to wrap any common cassandra operations into its own DSL.
